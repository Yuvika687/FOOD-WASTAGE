TIPS TO REDUCE FOOD WASTAGE

Plan Your Meals

Planning meals in advance helps you buy only what you need, reducing the chances of food going to waste.

Check Expiry Dates

Regularly check the expiry dates of your groceries to ensure you use them before they go bad.

Donate Unused Food

Consider donating excess food to local charities or shelters to help those in need.

Compost Food Scraps

Set up a compost system for fruit and vegetable scraps to reduce landfill waste and enrich your soil.

Proper Storage

Store fruits and vegetables properly to extend their freshness. Use airtight containers and refrigerate when necessary.

Use Leftovers Creatively

Transform leftovers into new dishes to avoid throwing away perfectly good food. Get creative with recipes!
